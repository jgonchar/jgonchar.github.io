About Hikes and Hops

Hi there! I'm Jessica, the writer, hiker, and beer-drinker behind Hikes and Hops.

I created Hikes and Hops to pair two of my favorite things: hiking and good beer. As a Seattle-native, I've loved being out in the Pacific Northwest since I can remember. I skipped the natty ice/budwiser/coors lite beer and went straight to craft beer, so I apologize in advance for being a bit of a beer snob. (Sort of.)

Hikes and Hops can help you plan a hike as well as where to stop afterwards for a cold brew. We also feature local breweries, tips for hiking, and more!

Comments? Question? I want to hear from you! Email me at jessica.gonchar@gmail.com.
